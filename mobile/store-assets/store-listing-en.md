## App Name
Maeil1Dok - Daily Bible Reading

## Short Description (80 characters max)

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Complete your Bible reading journey this year with Maeil1Dok!

Maeil1Dok is a Bible reading tracker app that helps you systematically read through the entire Bible. Get daily reading assignments, track your progress, and see how others are doing on their reading journey.

### Key Features

**Today's Reading**
- Get your daily Bible reading assignment
- Mark chapters as complete with one tap
- Catch up on missed days anytime

**Reading Plan**
- View your entire reading schedule at a glance
- See completed days and remaining assignments
- Jump to any day's reading instantly

**Progress Tracking**
- Monitor your personal reading progress
- View community-wide reading statistics
- Stay motivated with visual progress indicators

**Daily Video Content**
- Watch daily Bible meditation videos
- Gain deeper understanding of the text

**Group Features**
- Read together with your small group or community
- Group leaderboards add fun and accountability

**Community**
- Check the leaderboard to see reading progress
- Visit other participants' profiles

### Perfect For

- Anyone wanting to read the Bible from cover to cover
- Those who struggle to read the Bible alone
- People seeking a structured Bible reading approach
- Anyone needing community support for Bible reading
- Those wanting to build a daily devotional habit

### How to Use

1. Install the app and create an account
2. Check today's reading assignment
3. Read the Bible passage and mark it complete
4. Read daily to complete your Bible reading journey!

Sign in easily with Kakao or Google login.
